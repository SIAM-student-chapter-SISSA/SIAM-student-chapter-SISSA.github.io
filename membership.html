Membership

The registration can be completed by accessing the following webpage: https://my.siam.org/Login/?SSOL=Y .

Once on the site, “Create account” should be selected and the standard registration form completed with the required personal information.

After account creation, the large green “Join Now” button on the right-hand side should be clicked. To obtain the free student membership (otherwise subject to a fee), two sections must be completed.

First, the Education information section must be updated. Second, a Student Chapter Membership must be added by selecting “SISSA International School for Advanced Studies” as the institution, with the end date set to the expected graduation date.

Following this, the General Information section at the bottom of the page should be completed, and “Next” selected.

On the subsequent page, Free Graduate Student Membership – 15 Months should be chosen (the total should display as $0.00) and “Next” selected.

Next, two Activity Groups may be selected free of charge, according to personal interests, and the process continued by clicking “Next”.

In the Journal Products section, it should be noted that SIAM Journal on Mathematics of Data Science is currently the only journal available at no cost. Subscription may be selected if desired, while other journals remain subject to fees. The process then continues via “Next”.

Address details should be reviewed and confirmed, followed by “Next”.

On the Item Summary page, it should be verified that the total amount is Free, and “Next” should be selected.

Finally, “Complete Order” should be clicked to finalize the registration.
