Membership

The registration can be completed by accessing the following webpage: https://my.siam.org/Login/?SSOL=Y .
  1. Click Create account and complete the standard registration form with the required personal information.
  2. Click the large green Join Now button on the right-hand side and complete the two sections to obtain the free student membership (otherwise subject to a fee).
    • Update the Education information section.
    • Add a Student Chapter Membership by selecting SISSA International School for Advanced Studies as the institution, with the end date set to the expected graduation date.
  3. Complete the General Information section at the bottom of the page and select Next.
  4. Choose Free Graduate Student Membership – 15 Months (the total should display as $0.00), and select Next.
  5. Next, two Activity Groups may be selected free of charge, according to personal interests. Then, continue by clicking Next.
  6. In the Journal Products section, SIAM Journal on Mathematics of Data Science is currently the only journal available at no cost, while other journals remain subject to fees. Continue via Next.
  7. Review and confirm the Address details, then click Next.
  8. On the Item Summary page, verify that the total amount is Free, and select Next.
  9. Finally, click Complete Order to finalize the registration.
