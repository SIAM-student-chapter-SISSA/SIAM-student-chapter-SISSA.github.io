Membership

Join the SISSA SIAM Student Chapter and take part in our activities.

Become a member!

If you are a student at SISSA, you can join our SIAM Student Chapter. Membership is free and gives you the opportunity to participate in seminars, scientific events, networking activities, and chapter initiatives.

Members can also propose events, suggest speakers, and connect with other students and researchers interested in applied mathematics and computational science.

Join the chapter

Why join?

  • Attend seminars and workshops
  • Meet students and researchers
  • Propose scientific activities
  • Discover opportunities in applied mathematics
