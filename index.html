What is a SIAM Student Chapter?

A SIAM Student Chapter is a student organization affiliated with the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics, with the aim of promoting applied mathematics, computational science, and collaboration among students, researchers, and faculty members.

Our Chapter organizes seminars, courses, meetings, networking activities, and scientific events for students interested in applied mathematics, data science, numerical analysis, modeling, and related fields.

Become a member!

If you are a student at SISSA, you can join our SIAM Student Chapter. Membership is free and gives you the opportunity to participate in our activities, propose events, and connect with other students and researchers.

  • Meet students and researchers
  • Propose scientific activities
  • Discover opportunities in applied mathematics
